BioJS

Ambitious data visualization

BioJS is an open source Javascript library of components for creating ambitious visualizations of biological data. Modify and present your results in fast, easy and shiny new manner. Data visualisation has never been more beautiful.

What is BioJS?

BioJS builds a infrastructure, guidelines and tools to avoid the reinvention of the wheel in life sciences (= "Docker for Bio web components"). Our community builds modules than can be reused by anyone.
